Goat Simulator

Goat Simulator is a third-person perspective simulation game developed by Coffee Stain Studios. The player controls a goat aimed at doing as much damage as possible around an open world map.

The Sims 4

The Sims 4 is the fourth major title in simulation video game series, The Sims. It was developed by Maxis and The Sims Studio and was published by Electronic Arts. The game does not have a defined goal but allows the player to assume control over the lives of sims. Emotional state plays a large role in gameplay with effects on social interaction, user interface and personality all player a part in the sim’s health and wellbeing.
