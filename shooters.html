Counter-Strike Global Offensive

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a multi-player first-person shooter video game developed by Hidden Path Entertainment and Valve Corporation. It is the fourth game in the Counter-Strike series. The game puts two teams against each other: Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists. Both sides are tasked with eliminating the other while also completing separate objectives, the Terrorists, depending on the game mode, must either plant the bomb or defend the hostages, while the Counter-Terrorists must prevent the bomb from being planted or to rescue the hostages. There are six different game modes, all of which have distinct characteristics specific to that mode.

Overwatch

Over-watch is a team-based multi-player Online first-person shooter developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment.

Over-watch assigns players into two teams of six, with each player selecting one of 24 “heroes”, each having their own unique movement, attributes, and abilities. These “heroes” are divided into four categories: Offense, Defense, Tank and Support. Teams must work together to secure and defend control points on a map or escort a payload across the map in a limited amount of time.
